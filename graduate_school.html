Graduate School Library

The Graduate School Library houses a collection of materials primarily intended for graduate students and faculty members. They are given priority in using these resources.

Collections Available:

  • Graduate and Undergraduate Theses
  • Graduate School References

Borrowing Guidelines:

  • Priority Users – Graduate students and faculty members have priority access to the materials.
  • Undergraduate Access – Undergraduate students may borrow materials only if they are not in use by graduate students or faculty.

The Graduate School is located near the Filipiniana Collection Area.
