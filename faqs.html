Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Library Hours & Access

What are the library’s operating hours?

The library is open from 7:30 AM - 5:30 PM, Monday to Saturday.

Can I access the library on weekends?

The library is closed on Sunday.

Who can use the library?

The library is open to students, faculty, and staff of CTU-Argao. Visitors may request special access.

Borrowing & Returning Books

How many books can I borrow at a time?

Students can borrow up to 3 books at a time. The loan period depends on the book section.

What happens if I return a book late?

Late returns may result in penalties or required community service.

Can I renew a book online?

Currently, book renewal is only available in person at the library.

Library Resources & Facilities

What online resources are available?

The library provides access to OPAC, research databases, and the Online Resource Center.

Where is the discussion room located?

The discussion room is located near the Online Resource Center (ORC).

How do I access the library’s Wi-Fi?

The library does not offer free Wi-Fi for students. However, students can access the internet using the computers in the Online Resource Center.

Library Services

How do I ask for research assistance?

You can contact a librarian via Facebook or Gmail, or visit the library in person.

Can I book a study room online?

Study rooms can be requested online via Facebook/Gmail, but for faster assistance, it's best to visit the library in person.

Contact & Support

How do I contact a librarian or report technical issues?

You can contact the librarian for assistance or report technical issues through the following:

  • Facebook: Ctu Argao Library
  • Gmail: ctulibrary.argao@gmail.com
  • In Person: Visit the library during operating hours.
