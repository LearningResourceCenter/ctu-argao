Reading/Study Area

Our library provides a comfortable and quiet reading area designed to help students and faculty focus on research, studying, and academic work. The Reading Area is divided into different sections to cater to various study needs.

Study Areas

Our library has dedicated study areas on both the first and second floors:

  • Group study tables
  • Power outlets for laptops
  • Board Games Corner (Chess, Scrabble, & other educational games)
  • Casual Reading Area (For newspapers, magazines, and light reading)
  • Individual study desks
  • No group discussions allowed

Study Area Guidelines

To maintain a conducive study environment, please follow these guidelines:

  • Keep noise levels to a minimum.
  • No food and drinks allowed in the reading areas.
  • Respect other library users.
  • Use headphones when listening to audio materials.
  • Do not leave personal belongings unattended.
