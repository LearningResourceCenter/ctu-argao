Smart Classroom

The Smart Classroom is a technology-equipped space designed for official meetings, conferences, and academic discussions.

The Smart Classroom is located on the second floor, near the Reference Circulation area.

Usage Policies:

  • The Smart Classroom is primarily intended for official meetings, training, and academic conferences.
  • Other activities may be conducted only upon approval of the Campus Director through the endorsement of the College Librarian.
  • Audience size must not exceed the seating capacity of the room.
  • Users must handle the equipment with care and report any issues immediately.
  • Food and drinks are strictly prohibited inside the Smart Classroom to maintain cleanliness and protect the equipment.

Facilities & Equipment:

  • Interactive Smartboard for dynamic presentations.
  • High-definition projector and sound system.
  • Ergonomic seating for comfort and focus.
  • High-speed internet for seamless online collaboration.
  • Video conferencing tools for remote participation.

Reservation Guidelines:

  • Faculty and staff must book in advance through the library office.
  • Usage time should be followed to avoid scheduling conflicts.
  • Users must ensure proper shutdown of all equipment after use.
