Information Desk

The Information Desk is the primary help station of the CTU Argao Library. Located near the entrance, it serves as a hub for students, faculty, and visitors seeking assistance.

Services Offered

  1. General inquiries about library resources and services
  2. Clearance processing for students and faculty
  3. Assistance in locating books and digital resources
  4. Guidance on room reservations (Discussion Rooms, Smart Classroom)
  5. Technical support for OPAC and Electronic Bulletin Board
  6. Lost and found assistance

Location

The Information Desk is strategically located near the library entrance, right across the OPAC & Electronic Bulletin Board for easy access.

Operating Hours

  • Monday - Saturday: 7:30 AM - 5:30 PM
  • Sunday & Holidays: Closed
