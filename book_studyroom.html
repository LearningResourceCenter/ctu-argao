Book a Study Room

Looking for a quiet space to study or collaborate? Our study rooms are available for use.

How to Reserve a Study Room:

Note: Online booking is currently not available. You can reserve a room through the following methods:

  • In Person: Visit the library front desk and speak with a librarian.
  • Email: ctulibrary.argao@gmail.com
  • Facebook Page: Send us a message

Available Study Spaces:

  • Discussion Rooms (Research & Learning Hub 1 and 2): Suitable for small meetings or study groups (up to 9 people).
  • Smart Classroom: Can accommodate maximum of 65 people.

Study Room Guidelines:

  • Rooms are available on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • Maximum occupancy depends on room size.
  • Be mindful of noise levels to maintain a quiet environment.

For further inquiries, please contact us via email, Facebook, or visit the library in person.
